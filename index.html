Features

Everything your rotation needs

Designed for medical students navigating complex hospital shift systems.

Shift Booking

Browse booking cycles, check live capacity, and secure your slot before it fills — with built-in cooldown protection.

⭐

Points Tracking

Every attended shift earns points. View your monthly and all-time balance per unit at a glance.

👆

Basma Schedule

Set recurring attendance reminders per day of the week so you never miss a scheduled shift.

👥

Friends Rooms

Coordinate with classmates in private chat rooms linked to your unit duty schedule.

🔔

Smart Notifications

Instant push alerts when a booking cycle opens, a slot fills up, or your join request is approved.

📱

Home Screen Widget

See your next shift, countdown, and points balance without even opening the app.
