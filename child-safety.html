Safety

Child Safety Policy

Last updated: April 2026

RotaMate is committed to the safety and protection of all users. We have zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) content or behavior on our platform.

Age Requirement

RotaMate is designed for medical students and healthcare professionals. Users must be at least 16 years of age to create an account. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 16.

Our Commitment

We are committed to preventing the use of our platform for any form of child exploitation. Specifically:

  • We prohibit any content that sexually exploits or endangers children.
  • We prohibit the use of our platform to facilitate, promote, or engage in any form of child abuse.
  • We will immediately remove any content that violates this policy and permanently ban the offending account.
  • We will report any identified CSAE content or activity to the relevant law enforcement authorities, including the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) where applicable.

How to Report

If you encounter any content or behavior on RotaMate that you believe exploits or endangers a child, please report it immediately:

  • Email: rotamateapp@gmail.com

All reports are treated with the highest priority and strict confidentiality. We aim to review and act on all reports within 24 hours.

Enforcement

Violations of this policy will result in:

  • Immediate removal of the offending content
  • Permanent suspension of the user's account
  • Reporting to law enforcement authorities as required by applicable law
