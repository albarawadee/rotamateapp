Frequently Asked Questions

Why is my account pending verification?

After registration, an admin at your institution must verify your account before you can book shifts. This usually takes 1–2 business days. Contact your unit manager if it's taking longer.

How do I join a unit?

Go to the Units section and search for your unit by name or code. Tap "Request to Join" and wait for the manager to approve your request.

Why can't I book a shift?

Common reasons: the slot is full, the booking window hasn't opened yet, you have an active cooldown, or you've reached the monthly points limit for this unit.

How are points calculated?

Each shift slot has a point value set by the unit manager. Points are credited automatically when a booking is confirmed. Your balance is the sum of all your confirmed bookings.

I didn't receive a push notification

Make sure notifications are enabled for RotaMate in your phone's Settings app. If the issue persists, sign out and sign back in to refresh your push token.
