Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

RotaMate ("we", "our", or "us") is operated by Albara Wadee Ahmed Ghanem. This page explains how we handle your personal information when you use the RotaMate app.

Information We Collect

When you create an account or use the app, we collect:

  • Name — to identify you within your unit and to other members
  • Email address — for authentication and account recovery
  • Phone number — for account registration
  • Seat number / student ID — to verify your identity as a medical student
  • College and department — to assign you to the correct unit groups
  • Date of birth — for internal coordination features
  • Shift booking activity — your booked shifts, attendance records, and points

How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate you and manage your account
  • Display your profile to unit managers and fellow members
  • Process shift bookings and calculate points
  • Send push notifications for shift updates and messages
  • Provide customer support

Data Storage

Your data is stored on Supabase (supabase.com), a cloud platform. All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest.
